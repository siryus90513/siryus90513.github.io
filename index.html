About

The Taiwan-India Professional Expertise and Organization Cooperation Platform was established by the Taiwan-India Joint Research Center on Artificial Intelligence at National Chung Cheng University (CCU) and Indian Institute of Technology Ropar (IITR), Taipei Economic Cultural Center (TECC) in India, and the Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST). The main goal of this platform is to provide matching among professionals and organizations in and across Taiwan and India. Professionals include, but not limited to, university professors, R&D experts and scholars, industry experts, etc. Professional organizations include, but not limited to, universities, R&D institutions, government units, and industry manufacturers.
